Frequently asked questions

How many people fit in a raft?

Each raft can hold up to 5 people including our guide.

What shoudl I wear?

We recomed that you bring a swimsuit and a shirt you don't mind getting wet. We alos recomend bringing a pair of shoes that are waterproof as well.

What do rapid classifacations mean?

This is referring to the current and danger level based on conditions, such as speed of current and water levels. The levels go from level 1-6 with one being the most calm.
